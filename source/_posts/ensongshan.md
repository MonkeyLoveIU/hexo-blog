## Moments: A Sudden Impulse

While browsing Moments, I saw a friend share photos of climbing Mount Song.

I was instantly moved and messaged him saying I wanted to go too.

Unexpectedly, two days later he told me: there’s a group night hike up Mount Song on April 12th, ticket is 29 yuan. He reminded me to bring warm clothes and a flashlight, and said he’d handle the rest.

Another high school classmate joined us. We met at the designated spot around 7 p.m.

While waiting for the bus, I couldn’t resist buying a sausage to fill my stomach.

Mount Song is in Dengfeng, not far from Zhengzhou. I dozed off on the bus, and when I woke up, it was dark outside, with only dim lights nearby and distant city lights.

We arrived at the foot of the mountain after 10 p.m. The tour group gave everyone a trekking pole and a small plastic flashlight—not very bright, but better than nothing.

At the ticket gate, we refilled water and snacks, used the restroom, and got ready. At 11 p.m., the group entered in batches. Since they were checking insurance, we just blended in with the crowd and slipped through.

## Sausage: A Small Satisfaction on the Way

The night hike up Mount Song takes about three hours. We took it easy.

On the way, we saw a sausage stall and my friend bought one for each of us.

I’m usually not interested in sausages, but this time it tasted amazing. I even thought I should have one every time I hike.

## Midway Rest: An Encounter Under the Moonlight

Around 1 a.m., we reached halfway up and rested on a platform.

There were some girls nearby. My outgoing friend started chatting with them, and we even took a group photo together.

After resting, we continued on our way.

## Cold Wind at the Summit: Darkness Before Dawn

Around 3 a.m., we finally reached the summit. It was still early, but the top was already crowded.

We explored an abandoned building, then found some steps to sit and rest.

Mount Song was freezing at night. At first, the climb kept us warm, but gradually we got colder and curled up instinctively.

The mountain wind kept blowing. Luckily, I wore a windbreaker, or I wouldn’t have survived those three hours.

## Sea of Clouds: An Unexpected Surprise

The hard wait was rewarded with a breathtaking view—a rolling sea of clouds unfolded in the morning light, magnificent and awe-inspiring.

In that moment, all fatigue vanished, replaced only by admiration for nature’s beauty!

The photos couldn’t capture what I saw, but I was still satisfied.

## Morning Descent: Journey’s End

On the way down, there were breakfast stalls near the ticket gate. We ate a little and headed to the bus stop for the return trip.

I can’t remember which path we took down, only that there were sightseeing cars carrying tourists on the wide road.

The sky was especially bright, the morning light clear and gentle. Clouds floated and stretched across the sky.

Announcing the end of this journey!
